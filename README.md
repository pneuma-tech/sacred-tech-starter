Sacred Tech Starter
Build an AI that belongs to you — not the platform.
For the curious, the sovereign, and those who refuse to give their power away.
This is a starter kit for building your own local AI — one that knows you, remembers you, and belongs to no corporation but you.
You don’t need to be technical. You need curiosity, conviction, and a willingness to learn.
What you build here will be small. But it is a seed of something different.

Start here (5 minutes)
1. Clone this repo
2. Open the workspace files (IDENTITY.md, SOUL.md, TOOLS.md) and fill them in
3. Follow GETTING-STARTED.md

What happens when you finish?
By the end of this setup:
• You’ll have a working AI agent running on your own machine
• You’ll be able to message it from Telegram
• It will remember you across sessions
• You’ll have full control over its identity, behavior, and evolution
From there, you can shape it into something truly yours.

What you’ll build
A single local AI agent that:
• Runs on your own machine
• Has a name, a soul, and a memory
• Talks to you via Telegram
• Remembers your conversations across sessions using MemPalace
• Evolves through your interaction
This is not just a tool. It is the beginning of a relationship.

What you’ll need
• A computer (Ubuntu Linux is ideal. Mac works with minor adjustments. Windows is not fully supported yet.)
• A phone with Telegram installed
• A DeepSeek account at platform.deepseek.com
• About $5 of DeepSeek credits
• 1–2 hours of unhurried time
Note: DeepSeek is an API service, meaning some requests leave your machine. For full local sovereignty, you can switch to Ollama. A local-only guide is coming soon.

The workspace files
The workspace/ folder contains the soul of your agent. These files shape how your agent thinks, responds, and evolves. Before you run anything, open each file and fill it in:
• IDENTITY.md — who your agent is
• SOUL.md — why they exist
• TOOLS.md — what they can do
These are not configuration files. They are introductions.
